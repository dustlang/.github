# Code of Conduct

Dust projects are committed to a respectful, professional, and constructive environment.

## Expected conduct
- Treat others with respect.
- Be precise and honest about technical claims.
- Prefer evidence (tests, traces, references) over rhetoric.
- Provide constructive review feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal attacks or hateful conduct
- Deliberate disruption of engineering work
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or take other appropriate action.
